About

Sydney Kotlin User Group was officially founded in May 2017, however the first one-off event was technically in August 2016 as part of Android Australia User Group.

Ever since, it has been organised by a group of volunteers passionate about Kotlin, with help from sponsors, local companies opening their doors to organise events and JetBrains.

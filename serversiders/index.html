Welcome, Serversiders!

Serversiders, in association with Atlassian Engineering Nights, is a community of developers working on server-side applications, primarily in statically-typed languages, such as Java, Go, Kotlin, Rust and others.

Many groups focus on specific languages or frameworks. We understand that most real-world systems are built with a combination of related tools, so we aim to give people working on web backends, APIs and platforms a forum to discuss topics spanning multiple technologies.
